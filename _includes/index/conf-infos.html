The 8th edition of Incontro DevOps Italia (IDI2020) will take place in Bologna on Friday, April 28th. We are looking forward to meet you again this year!

Incontro DevOps is a 2-day conference about DevOps, Continuous Delivery, Cloud, Microservices, Automation, Testing, Big Data, Containers, Data Center Operating Systems, Software Operability, IT operating models and how to plan and organise IT Operations.

During the event, we will also host lightning talks and there will be an open space available. The day before the conference (27th of April 2020) our partners will present few workshops: more news will appear on Facebook and on Twitter (@incontrodevops).

Why join?

Whether you're a novice or an expert, grab the opportunity to learn something new from people using those technologies and methodologies every day.
